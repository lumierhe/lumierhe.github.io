Bulacan.

Summer in the Philippines is much awaited. It’s amazing to be in the Philippines during these times because the whole country is livelier and the tropical vibe is so much better. During summer time, lush tropical forests, rice fields, and long trips are what people look forward to the most.In Bulacan, you’ll find that there are so many resorts to choose from in case you need to go on a quick tour to somewhere quiet or just to enjoy a few dips in the pool. Bulacan is so close to Manila that it will only take an hour or two to get there. You can find your perfect resorts for your family, team building or barkada summer getaway here, in Bulacan.
